Have Confidence In Your Order



SeeFood was created by a group of students from Shoreland Lutheran High School in Kenosha, Wisconsin for our senior year engineering/computer science project. We spent many hours of class time and free time developing this site throughout the year. Our goal is to create a centralized menu site to help people with allergy or nutirition needs, or just for anyone looking to quickly find restaurant menus and see images of different menu items. Instead of being forced to search each individual restaurant website, if they even have one, our service's goal is to quickly compare the many restaurant items with allergen and nutritionial information along with images to help you make the best decision for your order.



SeeFood Creators: (left to right) John Schmidt, Ben Barrette, Derek Borchardt, Michael Zhang.



Disclaimer: Our information comes directly from the restaurants; however, cross-contamination and errors are always a possibility, and neither we nor the restaurants are responsible for any problems related to mishaps. Menu items and information are subject to change and these changes may not appear in our database immediately. As such, it is very important that each consumer uses their own discretion when ordering to avoid tragic accidents. Our tool is simply that: a tool, and so it should not be regarded as the sole source to inform a decision.
